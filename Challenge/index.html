TLabs accross the country had success in syntheizing meat from algae, fungi, and microorganisms found on plant roots. However, improbable beef is still searching for the elusive bacteria that will provide the perfect taste. We had a hypothesis that bacterial species to make synthetic beef, may be found on the human body the belly button. To test our hypothesis, we has sampled the navels of people across the country to identify bacterial species that colonize our belly tuoons. We built a dashboard that allow participants in the study to select their ID numbers to see which bacterial species live in their navels.
